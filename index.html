Places I've Lived: In Chronological Order

  1. Illinois
  2. Indiana
  3. Argentina
  4. Chicago
  5. Mexico
  6. Kansas City

Birth

Peoria, Illinois

1994: I was born at 12:23am in the sign of Virgo.

Notable event: My parents left me at home alone by accident when the forgot me in their rush to get to church.



Stomping Grounds

Granger and Elkhart, Indiana

2000 (Granger): 6 acres of land, an apple orchard, a sandbox the size of a pool, a tennis court, a basketball hoop, an abandoned and full tool shed, and unlimited time for imaginative play. I was homeschool for two years.

Notable event: My parents could leave me at home because our church was across the street from our house.


2002 (Elkhart): No friends, a few friends, a divorce, two school districts, four sports theatre, and loads of church..still.

Notable event: Lived in +10 houses/apartments.



Foreign Exchange

Catamarca, Argentina

2011: 8 1/2 months of freedom; religious, sexual, educational, mental. Tons of Fernet, Spanish language, and Argentinian culture absorbed.

Notable event: First beer, first sexual encounter, first joint, first foreign country, first time independent(ish).



College

Chicago

2013: 4 1/2 years. Theatre. Acting. Queer cultures. Big city livin'. Gentrification.

Notable event I tried commercial acting and realized that life is not for me.



A Wholistic Approach

Mazunte, Oaxaca, Mexico

2017: 1 1/2 months of dental work. 4 1/2 months of yoga and meditation practice. Life drew me toward a deeper sense of self through active-listening.

Notable event: Made a 4-day journey across 3 states and into another country to renew my tourist visa with no plan on how to get there and back.



Structure and Family

Kansas City, Missouri

2018: Coming back to the U.S. Parents.

Notable event: LC101 and yoga teacher-training.
